Science Board Paper

Hello wassup! How are you all tenthies...Hopefully your boards are Going well. Mine are like I have no words what to say what to not they are just ok. I love to be a part of Roop Nagar Public School but it's hard to leave it. I have a problem I don't confide in people that easily and making friends oh my god it's same as cracking JEE for a 8th class student as for an introvert. Sometimes I think why I have people in my life they must not be here common you all deserve better friends. I have only few people who I can call real friends but nobody knows me Neither my parents, my family nor my friends. And if they know about how much I suffered they don't know me who is me? Who is there? Huh all so complicated...

The thing is that I went to Pune for a ROBOTICS COMPETITION there we were total of 5 students 4 of same class. We played several games like the most popular Truth and Dare, UNO, etc. I felt a change in myself I confided in a person I had never talked before as a friend and out of no where I opened my heart in front of him. I don't know why (But everything happens for a reason) I told him everything like seriously. He knows much about me. The things I never talked of I shared with him and almost everything. It was a dream of me to have a good friend because I don't talk to people and share what's going on in my mind that easily to anyone, But yes I told him everything like what happened with me in past and the future I am worried about and all.

But today was my Science Board Examination not only mine but of every student studying in CBSE class 10. One of my friend the one with whom I for the first time shared something in class 9 like i opened myself for the first time in front of somebody that How I am feeling what's going on in my mind and all. Mumma told me that she saw her coming out of the center when her face was showing everything, I also met her before the Examination and she was really too much anxious. I don't know exactly what I felt at that moment but all the ssatisfaction like the one I have after successfully crossing this valley of Science it all vanished and I became too much worried. I texted her as soon as possible for me. And gathered courage to call her. Its dramatic that you can't call your friend and talk normally. I know this but I just hate myself. Cause I have reason for this too: THE DARK PAST......
